Q550 Models in Cognitive Science, Prof. John K. Kruschke

Exercises, Week 1, Due Thursday January 17, 2001.

Please do the four activities in the linked web page. Each activity involves first participating as a subject in a brief, on-line experiment. Then you examine your data and investigate to what extent a simple model fits your data. Average results from a large number of participants are linked with each activity. These average results may help clarify the empirical phenomena being modeled, after you have gone through the experiment yourself.
