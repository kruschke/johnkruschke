Additional Exercises for Week 4

  1. Normal distribution as a model for residuals. Suppose we have a set of scores
    4, 5, 5, 6, 6, 6, 7, 8, 8.
    We believe that a good model for the population from which these scores were drawn is a central tendency, mu, with normally distributed deviations with mean zero and variance sigma**2. Formally, this can be written
    Yi = mu + ei, with ei drawn from N(0,sigma**2)
    where Yi is the i-th score, mu is the central tendency of the population, ei is the residual of the i-th score, and N(0,sigma**2) is a normal distribution with mean zero and variance sigma**2.

    What is our best estimate of the parameter mu?

    What is our best estimate of the parameter sigma**2?
