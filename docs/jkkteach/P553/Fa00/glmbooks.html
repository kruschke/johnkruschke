Some textbooks that explain the general F ratio...

The perspective explained in lecture, regarding the general F-ratio as the rate of increase in error as parameters are jettisoned, is described in these textbooks:

Maxwell, Scott E. and Delaney, Harold D. (1990).
Designing experiments and analyzing data: A model comparison perspective.
Belmont, CA: Wadsworth Publishing Co.

Judd, Charles M. and McClelland, Gary H. (1989).
Data analysis: A model-comparison approach.
San Diego, CA: Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, Publishers.

If you find other textbooks with similar approaches, please do let me know (kruschke@indiana.edu).
