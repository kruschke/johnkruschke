Weekly Topics

This page lists week-by-week topics. Homework assignments might be edited and adjusted until the last minute. Therefore, do not print (or do) any homework assignment until after the lecture of the day it is officially assigned. In the topics listed below, chapter numbers preceded by "AA" refer to the Aron & Aron textbook. Overarching course goals are described in the syllabus. An outline of topics with dates is provided in the schedule overview.
