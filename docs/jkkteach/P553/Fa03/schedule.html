Schedule Overview

The schedule below is approximate, and will flex and bend as the course progresses. The order of topics will remain the same, but the exact dates for each topic might change. Homework is assigned on Tuesdays, and is due the following Tuesday, when another assignment is given (except on exam weeks and Thanksgiving break). This schedule is designed so that the Thursday lab sessions can be partially devoted to discussions of the current week's homework.

Homework assignments will be edited and adjusted until the last minute. Therefore, do not print (or do) any homework assignment until after the lecture of the day it is officially assigned!
