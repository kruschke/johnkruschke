P553 Statistics in Psychology, Prof. Kruschke

Additional Exercises for Week 6

  1. Find a provocative, controversial claim in the print media (book, newspaper, magazine, pamphlet, world wide web) that is amenable to statistical analysis. Very briefly describe the claim, and briefly explain how statistical analysis could address it. Attach a copy of the document (or the page of the document) in which the claim is made clearly and blatantly.

  2. Suppose someone flips a coin 2 times and gets 2 heads. What is the best (point) estimate of p=pr(heads)? What is the 95% confidence interval for the estimate of p?

  3. Suppose someone flips a coin 3 times and gets 2 heads. What is the best (point) estimate of p=pr(heads)? What is the 95% confidence interval for the estimate of p?
