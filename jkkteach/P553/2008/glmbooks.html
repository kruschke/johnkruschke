Some textbooks that explain the general F ratio...

The perspective explained in lecture, regarding the general F-ratio as the rate of increase in error as parameters are jettisoned, is described in these textbooks:

  • Maxwell, S. E. & Delaney, H. D. (2004). Designing Experiments and Analyzing Data, 2nd Ed.. Mahwah, NJ: Erlbaum.

    P554 for Spring semester will use the Maxwell and Delaney textbook (above).

  • Judd, Charles M. and McClelland, Gary H. (1989). Data analysis: A model-comparison approach. San Diego, CA: Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, Publishers.
