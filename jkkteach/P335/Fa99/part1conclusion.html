THEME: Perception is an active, constructive process
of inference based on ambiguous stimuli.

  • Perceptual ambiguities and illusions highlight this theme.

  • Perception builds up complex representations of the world through various processes

    • topic of pattern recognition emphasized representations

An Implication: Human perception is naturally prone to error.
We must tolerate unavoidable error,
while not accepting irresponsibility.
Cognitive psychology can help us draw the line between excusable error and negligence.
