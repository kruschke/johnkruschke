Aug. 28:
The paper copies of the Late Policy contain an error regarding the maximum number of points possible for late homework or papers. The paper copy correctly states that one third of the points are deducted off the top for homework or papers handed in up to a week late without an excuse, but the paper copy incorrectly states the maximum point values. The correct points are as follows: For homework handed in less than a week late, a maximum of 12 points (= 2/3 x 18) is possible. For homework handed in more than a week late, no points are awarded. For papers handed in less than a week late, a maximum of 18 points (= 2/3 x 27) is possible. For papers handed in more than a week late, no points are awarded.
