  2. 10.3 (p.518). The idea here is that the search always selects the feature with the least numerous distractors; i.e., it's not probabilistic. Nevertheless, the selection time is not constant for some reason. What's a plausible reason? (Hint: maybe it takes time to suppress attention to unselected items, and the more items there are, the longer it takes.
