Comments regarding homework assignments

from Matthias Schuetz

  • Email is preferred. Send homeworks in ASCII format by email to mscheutz@indiana.edu Write in the subject line: Q550-homework number, so my automated mailing system will file them correctly. If possible, don't use attachments (as it is more complicated to include them in replies if I need to comment on your answers).

  • Type your homeworks if possible, otherwise, please, write legibly and with clear organization. It is in your best interest, if I can read your arguments.

  • Don't turn in screenshots of single epochs of training. Turn in only the results (if so indicated). Try to be succinct and short in your answer, but elaborte it where necessary (e.g., if the question asks to describe the difference of two networks, take your time to include all essential differences, also explaining why they differ).

  • If a problem consists of a "Yes/no" together with a "Why or why not?" question, please, state your answer (yes or no) clearly and then give the arguments supporting it. I will from now on look in particular for analytical arguments that rely on the functionality (i.e., the math) of the network. General answers such as "The network could not learn the problem, because the data did not permit it" will not earn credit!
